About POLISH
“POLISH” is a project being done for a For-Profit Organization in Kochi, Kerala called “Oorjja”(https://www.oorjja.org/). This organization is aimed at providing a livelihood for the people above 15 years of age, with speech & hearing disability,who enrol with them. Oorjja is dedicated to finding the right skill of the student and providing training to polish these skills so that they can be directed to the right job that is suitable for them. As part of this vision, our team at Ramaiah Institute of Technology, was entrusted with the task of building a game based application which will find out the inherent logical skills of a student enrolled with Oorjja.
